3. Education in Pakistan – Challenges and Hope
Education in Pakistan faces many challenges such as lack of resources, outdated curricula, and inequality between urban and rural areas. However, recent years have seen growing awareness and reforms. More NGOs, digital platforms, and government initiatives are working to improve access to quality education, especially for girls and underprivileged communities.
